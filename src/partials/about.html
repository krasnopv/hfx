About Hive FX

Hivefx is a Visual Effects global collective known for Producing High Quality VFX work for Feature Films, Episodic, Design, TV,  and Experiential Advertising.

Our collective has been built over the last 20 years, establishing strong, reliable and trusted partners that deliver exceptional work for our clients, time and time again. HiveFX is in essence a formalisation of this collective, giving our clients a single point of contact. Not only that, we have streamlined our collectives’ pipeline and are able to run projects across multiple studios seamlessly.

Hive FX Services

Our visual effects services include; Photorealistic CG Environments built from the ground up, to Expansive Blue/Green Screen Set Extension,  Realtime 360 environments for large LED volumes and Virtual Production sets, 3d Modelling, Retouch-Prep, Rotoscopy, Matte Paint, Compositing, Animation, Matchmove, and Rotomation. 

We work with projects of all sizes and offer flexible solutions tailored to your specific needs. This includes providing a team of artists to seamlessly integrate into your existing team and pipeline. Alternatively, we can take on entire shots or elements that are more suitable for outsourcing.

Data security and privacy is paramount to how we operate our business. Our collective can provide secure network and data management approved by The Trusted Partner Network.
